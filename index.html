The Anarchist Folk Band

The Anarchist Folk Band was founded in 2018 by Nick Thornton, Sarah Beth Tanner and Mike Mazzocca following a very successful run of Haymarket: A New Folk Musical, produced by Underscore Theatre Company in Chicago, Illinois. Having discovered a fountain of talent within the ranks of their ensemble, The Anarchist Folk Band was formed as a way to continue to create beautiful stories and music together while giving back to the community.
